How to Create A encoded video for a website using Adobe media encoder

This will make the file smaller which causes the page to load faster

Step 1:

Open Media Encoder Drag in your video and click on "h.264"

Step 2:

Change the preset to facebook 720p HD

Step 3:

Copy the following settings under "video"

Step 4:

Copy these settings under "audio" and click "Ok"

Step 5:

Move the Video to your Assets folder

Step 6:

Open Brackets and type the following

<video width = "720" height = "480" controls> <source src="Assets/placeholder.mp4" type="video/mp4"> <p>Video Not Found/Browser Doesn't Support HTML5</p> </video>
